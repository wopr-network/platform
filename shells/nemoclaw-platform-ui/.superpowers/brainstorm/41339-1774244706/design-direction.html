NemoPod Design Direction

What visual identity should NemoPod have? Pick the vibe that fits.

NemoPod
NVIDIA NEMO • AI AGENTS

A: NVIDIA Green

Lean into the NVIDIA NeMo heritage. Black background, NVIDIA green (#76B900) as primary accent. Technical, GPU-forward, developer-focused. Says "this runs on serious hardware."

NemoPod
AI AGENT PODS

B: Amber Terminal

Keep the current terminal aesthetic but own it. Warm amber (#F59E0B) on black. Retro-futuristic terminal feel. Distinct from WOPR's green but same family. "Your AI, your pod."

NemoPod
INTELLIGENT AI AGENTS

C: Modern Indigo

Break from the terminal aesthetic entirely. Clean dark UI with indigo/violet accents. Sans-serif. Modern SaaS feel like Linear or Vercel. "AI agents for everyone, not just hackers."

NemoPod
DEPLOY • CHAT • SCALE

D: Cyan / Neon

Dark + cyan (#22D3EE). Sci-fi pod aesthetic. Monospace. Think Tron meets terminal. "NemoClaw instances are pods you deploy and talk to." Cold, sharp, futuristic.
